EMBERS 2023

P A College of Engineering, Dept of CSE organizes 2 days embeddathon and project exhibition under Embed Club on 28th and 29th of April 2023. Embers is a national level technical fest which aims to solve problems and give a platform to showcase the project. Embers will be conducted at PACE campus.

  • Winners will be announced the second day.

Day 1 Embeddathon :

Embers Embeddathon is a flagship event which aims to solve real world problems mainly in 4 domains they are,

  • Agriculture
  • Healthcare
  • Education
  • Environment
  • Home Automation

Rules:

  • Each team has to select any one topic from the above mentioned.
  • Open for all students.
  • Carry all the components and requirements for the event.
  • All work should be done during the embeddthon.
  • Teams can use an idea they had before the event.
  • Innovative projects are entertained.
  • Teams should stop working once the time is up, however they can do small modification on next day.
  • Breaking of rules will lead to disqualification.

Important Note:

  • Partial outputs are considered.
  • All participants will get certificate of participation.
  • Time allotted for working on the project is 9:00 am to 5:00 pm on 28th April 2023.
  • On 29th 9:00 am team should be ready for the presentation.
  • Decision made by the judges will be final and binding.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Innovation
  • Design
  • Efficiency
  • Relevance with practical need

Note: Winners will be awarded with exciting prizes.
