EMBEDATHON

P A College of Engineering, Dept of CSE organizes 2 days embeddathon and project exhibition under Embed Club on 28th and 29th of April 2023. Embers is a national level technical fest which aims to solve problems and give a platform to showcase the project. Embers will be conducted at PACE campus.

Rules:

  • Students can participate as individual or team.
  • In a team there can only be 4 members
  • Registration fee for a team is ₹ 200 irrespective of the team members count
  • Students participating for embeddathon will have a chance to attend project competition for free.
  • Each team will have allotted space if team any other assistance they have to inform in prior.
  • Evaluation of Embeddathon and project exhibition will be separate 
  • Winners will be announced the second day
